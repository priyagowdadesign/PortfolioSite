Avid Saver

Avid Saver is a Mobile App that helps to save money for an upcoming expense.

Avid Saver App was inspired by everyday people who strive to achieve their goals - both big and small through financial means. Avid Saver makes a humble attempt to help people create custom-tailored plans so they can grow and realize their financial goals. The word 'Avid' alludes to the fact that most users who use the service are enthusiastic and motivated to work towards their objectives.
