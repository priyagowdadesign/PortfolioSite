About me

I'm Priya, a Software Engineer turned UI/UX designer, passionate about creating aesthetic and intuitive UI design with seamless usability. I have a strong background in Software Engineering that helps me understand Software systems and processes from a technical perspective. I hope to leverage this to design realistic products that are possible within the scope and technical framework of a project. My work experience has helped me develop a keen eye for detail and an appreciation of the value of good design. My ability to speak the developers' language helps me act as a bridge between designers and developers.

I have a Career foundry certification in UI Design and a Bachelor's degree in Engineering with subsequent 9 years of experience as a Software Developer. Some of the employers/clients that I have worked with include Mercedes-Benz Financial Services, Infosys, J P Morgan Chase & Accenture.

When I am not working, I like to travel with my family & friends, do creative photography & video editing. I also have an active Instagram profile to share my experiences.
